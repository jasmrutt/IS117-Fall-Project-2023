Education

New Jersey Institute of Technology

At new Jersey Institute of Technology, Jasmin Rutter has taken a variety of courses. To name a few, she has taken networking, website development, and management. She was originally attracted to NJIT because of the diverse majors they offered. Not many colleges have offered Information Technology as a major and she was interested in pursuing that interest. Over the course of her time at NJIT thus far, she is proud to say that she has learned a great deal of information. Below is a detailed description of all the courses I have taken each semester.
